This site is being decommissioned. The legacy site may become unstable or unavailable at any time.

Where to Go

After more than 20 years of serving the pediatric cardiology community, the Parameterz z-score calculators have moved to a new home at Rady Children's Hospital. The new site includes all the familiar calculators with improved features and ongoing support.

While the calculators have moved, this domain will continue to host other projects.
